Pizza Recipe

Welcome to our ultimate guide to making a delicious homemade pizza from scratch! Whether you're a seasoned chef or a kitchen novice, this recipe will walk you through each step to create a pizza that's bursting with flavor and topped with your favorite ingredients. From kneading the perfect dough to crafting a savory sauce and layering on the toppings, you'll have everything you need to enjoy a restaurant-quality pizza right at home. Let's get started on making a meal that everyone will love!

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110째F or 45째C)
  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

For the Pizza Sauce:

  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons oregano
  • 2 teaspoons basil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Toppings:

  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Activate the Yeast:
    • In a large bowl, combine the warm water, yeast, and sugar. Stir gently and let sit for 5-10 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy.
  2. Prepare the Dough
    • Add 2 cups of flour, olive oil, and salt to the yeast mixture. Stir to combine.
    • Gradually add more flour, 1/2 cup at a time, until the dough begins to come together and pulls away from the sides of the bowl.
  3. Knead the Dough:
    • Turn the dough onto a lightly floured surface. Knead for about 5-7 minutes until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  4. Let the Dough Rise:
    • Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, turning it to coat with oil. Cover with a damp cloth or plastic wrap.
    • Let the dough rise in a warm place for about 1-1.5 hours, or until it has doubled in size.
  5. Combine Ingredients:
    • In a medium bowl, mix together the tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  6. Adjust Seasoning:
    • Taste the sauce and adjust seasoning as necessary.
  7. Preheat the Oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.
  8. Shape the Dough:
    • Punch down the risen dough and divide it into two equal portions for two pizzas.
    • On a lightly floured surface, roll out one portion of dough into a 12-inch circle.
  9. Prepare the Pizza Base:
    • Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza peel or baking sheet lined with parchment paper if using a pizza stone, or directly to a greased baking sheet.
    • Brush the dough lightly with olive oil.
  10. Add the Sauce and Toppings:
    • Spread a layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
    • Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the sauce.
    • Add your desired toppings.
  11. Bake the Pizza:
    • Transfer the pizza to the oven. If using a pizza stone, slide the pizza and parchment paper onto the stone.
    • Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  12. Serve:
    • Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
